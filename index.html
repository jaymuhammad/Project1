Cheerity4All is a non-profit organization that serves disadvantaged men, women, and children of all ages from all walks of life in the Louisville and surrounding areas. Louisville has a very culturally diverse population and Cheerity4All employs a culturally diverse staff to better serve the demographic that our non-profit attempts to reflect through our staff.
