Mission

Our mission is to reach out to every group from every culture along with Louisville's Urban and suburban children to provide and give access to decent clothing and well balanced meals to those youth who may not be receiving them. As studies have shown, proper and healthy diets lead to healthy minds giving our City's children the opportunity to obtain greatness and reach their full potential.
